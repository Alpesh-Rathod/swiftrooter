Homeowner Tips
3 Things to Check
This Month
01

Inspect Under-Sink Cabinets

Look for moisture, discoloration, or musty odors — early signs of a slow leak that could escalate into mold damage or warped cabinetry.

02

Flush Your Water Heater

Sediment buildup reduces efficiency and shortens the lifespan of your unit. Flushing it annually saves you money and prevents surprise failures.

03

Know Where Your Shutoff Is

In a plumbing emergency, every second counts. Know where your main water shutoff valve is and make sure it turns freely before you need it.
